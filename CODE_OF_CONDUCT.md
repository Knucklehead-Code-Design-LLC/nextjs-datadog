# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and constructive environment
for everyone participating in this project, regardless of background,
experience, identity, or ability.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific.
- Discuss ideas and observable behavior rather than attacking people.
- Give and receive technical feedback in good faith.
- Respect privacy, disclosure boundaries, and contributor attribution.
- Help keep issues and pull requests focused and accessible.
- Accept maintainer decisions and disengage constructively when asked.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Abusing reporting, review, or communication systems.
- Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
  community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, issues, or
other participation that violates this policy. Depending on severity and
history, maintainers may issue a private warning, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban a participant.

Report conduct concerns privately to
[dev@knuckleheadcodedesign.com](mailto:dev@knuckleheadcodedesign.com). Reports
will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible. Maintainers who are
the subject of a report will not participate in resolving it when another
maintainer is available.

Good-faith reports will not result in retaliation. Knowingly false or abusive
reports may themselves violate this policy.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when a person is publicly representing
the project or its community.
